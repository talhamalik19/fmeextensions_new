Description

Provide complete product info to improve conversion rate. Attach guides, tutorials, manuals, warranties, policies, and other relevant information to your product and CMS pages with Magento 2 Product Attachments. Upload PDF, DOCs, XLS or any other file type and add custom icons to make them look attractive.

  • Attach multiple files on product & CMS pages
  • Separate files download page with categories
  • Attach PDF, Docs, Zip, links, videos or any other file type
  • Display attractive icons
  • Restrict attachments by customer groups & store view
  • Set download limit & display download counter
  • Drag & drop to upload files
